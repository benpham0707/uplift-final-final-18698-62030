## ðŸ’¡ Key Design Decisions

### Why 6 Dimensions?
Based on holistic review research - covers:
1. **Academic Excellence** (GPA, rigor) - Most important per NACAC
2. **Leadership & Initiative** - 18% more common among top admits
3. **Intellectual Curiosity** - Predicts college success
4. **Community Impact** - Service and social responsibility
5. **Authenticity & Voice** - Uses PIQ scores as signal
6. **Future Readiness** - Goal clarity and preparation

### Why 9 LLM Calls?
- **Not arbitrary** - Based on PIQ narrative workshop (also 9 calls)
- **Proven architecture** - Parallel batching optimizes performance
- **Each has specific job** - Holistic â†’ Deep Dive â†’ Synthesis â†’ Guidance
- **Balance depth vs cost** - More would be redundant, fewer would miss nuance

### Why Tier-Based Rubrics?
- **Developmental stages** - Students grow through tiers (not arbitrary scores)
- **Strategic pivots** - Clear action to reach next tier
- **Calibrated to data** - Each tier maps to school fit
- **Prevents inflation** - Explicit anchors and examples
