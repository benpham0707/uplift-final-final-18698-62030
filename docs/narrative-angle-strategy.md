## Angle Generation Principles

### 1. The Least Obvious Path
Don't connect A â†’ B (expected)
Connect A â†’ Z (unexpected but logical)

**Example**:
- Expected: Robotics â†’ Engineering career
- Unexpected: Robotics â†’ Understanding human vulnerability

### 2. The Counter-Intuitive Flip
Find what SEEMS wrong but reveals deeper truth

**Examples**:
- "Silence taught me more than words"
- "Losing was my greatest victory"
- "Ignorance became my superpower"

### 3. The Philosophical Lens
Ask: What does this reveal about:
- Human nature?
- How systems work?
- The nature of knowledge?
- Ethics and responsibility?

### 4. The Sensory Anchor
Ground abstract ideas in concrete sensory details

**Examples**:
- "The smell of burnt circuits"
- "The weight of a missing semicolon"
- "The texture of uncertainty"

### 5. The Universal Insight
How does this specific experience reveal something true about ALL humans/systems?
